BOB ROSS PAINTING CLASSES WITH PAUL AND TERRI

About Us

With more than 15 years experience in teaching Art Classes using the Bob Ross Painting Technique, Paul and Terri provide classes for budding artists in a number of locations in East and West Sussex, Hampshire and Surrey. The Home Studio is based in Worthing and is used in conjunction with the other venues.

Whilst we mainly offer tuition to adults, budding artists under 18 years old are welcome to join in provided they are accompanied throughout the duration of the class by an adult or guardian. The classes are designed to offer everyone a great environment to start and develop their skills in painting with oils. Using the world famous Bob Ross Technique all the materials, tuition and encouragement needed to get started with painting can be found at the classes. Bob Ross Painting classes with Paul and Terri are for beginners and improvers. With classes of up to 12 painting at the regular venues and smaller classes of up to 4 at the home studio. Private groups and individual tuition is also available.

Paul and Terri started painting in response to seeing 'The Joy of Painting' program presented by Bob Ross on television many years ago. The idea that anyone could paint was so appealing that Terri and Paul decided to enrolled in the Bob Ross teacher training program in the Netherlands initially to see if they could progress their skills. This quickly moved on to the idea of classes in the UK to help other experience the joy of painting. Their love of painting and passion for teaching have developed into the classes using the Bob Ross technique over the past ten years.
