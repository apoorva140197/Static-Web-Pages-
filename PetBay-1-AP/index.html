About Us


Pet Bay Ltd is a pet store based in Mapplewell, Barnsley providing a fantastic range of pet food, wild bird food, pet supplies and accessories. We are available for you to shop in store, online or you can order over the phone with the ability to reserve and collect or have home delivery . We offer FREE HOME DELIVERY to all addresses in Barnsley, South Yorkshire, with a small charge for addresses nationally.

There is an in store loyalty discount scheme available Stocking a wide range of pet foods including James Wellbeloved, Royal Canin, Dr Johns and Felix, we are sure that we can meet all your pets needs. Our customers come to us from all the corners of the UK with many providing recommendations of our excellent customer service.
