About Us

Operating from Caerphilly, Autolectrix provides auto electrical repairs and accessories, for all makes, to clients throughout Caerphilly, Cardiff and surrounding areas. A fully mobile service is offered at no extra cost Autolectrix has a growing customer base, located in and around Caerphilly, including private clients and trade businesses.

With mobile facilities, the company delivers a professional electrical service to the general public or trade customers. The service is designed to be cost effective with competitive pricing, helping clients to get back on the road with the minimum of fuss and inconvenience.

As well as car, commercial, plant equipment, marine and agricultural vehicles, a mobile service for caravans and motorhomes is also offered.A variety of services is available, to include (but not limited to)alarms,immobilisers, in-car entertainment, accessories, dash-cams etc. Latest Launch, Autel, Tech 2, Renault Clip, Peugeot Planet, Citroen Lexia diagnostic equipment for Engine Management, Traction Control, Airbag and ABS faults. Diagnose only or diagnose and repair.
