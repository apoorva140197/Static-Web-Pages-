AIRCRAFT MANAGEMENT

We bring you the confidence and security that comes only from working with an industry leader: As London Executive Aviation, and now as Luxaviation UK, we have been efficiently and safely operating business aircraft for more than 20 years.
We operate a diverse fleet across the spectrum of business aviation, which means we will already be familiar with any aircraft you wish us to manage, from an entry-level jet to a long- range aircraft. We have strong relationships with all the business jet manufacturers globally.
We maintain the personal touch at all times. When you place your private jet with us for aircraft management, you are immediately allocated a single, direct contact – effectively your ‘PA’ at Luxaviation UK.
Your personal contact is just the beginning. Our entire team is at your disposal, including our 24/7 operations room.
We also offer a 24/7 rapid response service for technical issues. Our in-house aircraft management engineering team guarantees the highest standards of maintenance support. Furthermore, should you need to travel when your aircraft is undergoing maintenance, we will provide you with another aircraft from our fleet.
We ensure all the aircraft we operate comply with international safety standards and regulations, including the requirements of the European Aviation Safety Agency (EASA).
By placing your aircraft for management with us, you will be able to enjoy considerable savings through our special negotiated prices on fuel, handling, hangarage and airport fees, as well as saving on insurance premiums by adding your aircraft to our group policy.
We will add your aircraft to our worldwide air operator’s certificate (AOC) and, if you wish, make the aircraft available for third-party charter, helping you generate considerable financial returns to offset your own operating overheads and finance costs.
From flight and cabin crew through to catering and cleaning support, we can find you the perfect team for your business aviation needs. Every detail matters; we make sure every job is carried out to the highest professional standards in aircraft management.
With complete financial transparency in our services, we will check every expense relating to your aircraft, always finding the most cost-effective course of action.

PRIVATE JET CHARTER

A scheduled flight has become something to endure, with delays and congestion robbing you of control and much needed time. It doesn’t have to be this way.Flying with Luxaviation UK is a breath of fresh air. You’re in charge of your private jet charter flight - our schedule works around you.
We go when you’re ready. No more queues, no more hassles, no more endless delays. Be whisked through security and airborne in as little as ten minutes. Focus on what matters while we take care of the rest - getting you there quickly, quietly and on time.
Fly from your choice of airfield, perhaps only minutes from your home or office. Arrive at a quiet terminal close to your destination, cutting ground transportation to a minimum. Once your meeting is finished your private jet aircraft will be waiting. And if things overrun, that’s no problem - we’re at your service. Whether you fly seldom or often, alone or with a group, our courteous team will look after your every need.
