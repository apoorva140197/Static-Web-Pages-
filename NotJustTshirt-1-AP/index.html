About Us

Not Just T-Shirts offer a full printing and embroidery service in Chesterfield so you can get almost everything done under one roof. Our friendly and helpful team will advise you about our products and services making the ordering process as simple as possible.Our customers range from individuals, businesses, clubs and teams, councils and schools.Contact us now for a quote and we will do our best to help you.We are a one stop print shop in Chesterfield offering Embroidery, Garment Printing, Large Format Printing, Sublimation Printing, Screen Printing.

We do most of this in-house or for some work we use some of the best specialist printer available. Once you contact us we can provide quotes, offer advise, send our brochures and then when an order is placed we can look at providing samples or mock ups for you to approve. Whilst we are able to compete with larger companies on price we can still offer the same service for customer wanting small or one off orders.

At Not Just T-Shirts we can supply and embroidery or print onto a wide range of garment or products, most popular are Polo Shirts, T-Shirts and Hoodies but we can also provide corporate items such as Bar Shirts and Business clothing. We also offer large format printing for vinyl banners, posters and such like.

We have just started to offer business stationary which includes business cards, compliment slips ect. For promotional printing we design and print flyers, mugs, pens and anything you can think of really and if we can print it we know somebody who does usually. We have a small team here at Not Just T-Shirts but when needed we can call in the cavelry and use our hired help to up production so nobody is ever let down. Not Just T-Shirts is owned by friends Andy and Mark. We work with individuals, companies, small businesses, groups and clubs. We have completed work for some of the countries biggest companies such as Tesco and Asda but also work with many individuals on a daily basis.
