About Us

With over 18 years experience in the accounts, book-keeping, finance and taxation sector, Amin and Co; Accountants offers solution for all accounts, book-keeping, taxation, VAT and payroll matters to customers throughout the local area.

The firm is located in Manchester and Bolton and well established and equipped with the latest knowledge to provide its clients the best possible services. All services are provided at very competitive price with a high quality standards With a well equipped with latest knowledge and fully qualified staff, the company is able to offer a professional accountancy, book-keeping, taxation, VAT and payroll services to businesses all over the Greater Manchester area.

The service is intended to be cost effective, helping clients to increase revenue and tax saving.Amin and Co; Accountants has a growing network of clients, situated throughout the UK, including limited companies, partnership, sole traders and self employed.

With a team that includes qualified and experienced staff members, Amin and Co; Accountants has a professional team, qualified to a high standard to serve clients to the best of their abilities.The firm is managed by qualified and experienced accountants who has been involved in the accounting industry for many years.Amin and Co; Accountants is a member of Greater Manchester Chamber of Commerce.
